Featured Accommodations

Toka Leya Camp

Perched on raised decks overlooking the Zambezi River in the Mosi-oa-Tunya National Park, seven miles upstream from Victoria Falls, is this upmarket tented camp run by conservation pioneers Wilderness Safaris. Toka Leya offers memorable views from its riverside decks and a Safari-lite taste of the African wilderness.

Mukwa River Lodge

A stylish stay near the Victoria Falls, but there's much more to Mukwa than just location and luxury. From harvesting from the kitchen garden to sampling novel foraged ingredients; shopping in local markets to fireside chats with the hands-on owners, it offers a more authentic, layered experience.

View Details
The Royal Livingstone

A historic five-star with bucket-loads of atmosphere on the banks of the Zambezi, with shady verandas, manicured lawns, roaming wildlife and open fires. Free access to Victoria Falls (a 15-minute walk) means you can come and go as you please, plus plenty of adrenaline-pumping activities to keep you occupied.
